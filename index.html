Storymap AI Plugin Description

The Story Mapping AI plugin is designed to enhance the reading experience for readers by providing character relationship maps that improve their understanding of the story's dynamics. This tool allows readers to visualize the connections between characters, making it easier for them to follow the narrative and appreciate the intricacies of the relationships within the novel. Whether you're a passionate reader looking to delve deeper into a story or a student of literature seeking a better comprehension of character dynamics, this plugin can be a valuable resource for improving your reading experience.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and take the responsibility of protecting your information seriously. Our privacy policy outlines the types of information we might collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

We only collect data necessary for the operation of our plugin. This includes diagram data generated within the plugin. We do not collect any personal identification information.

Data Usage

The data collected is solely used for improving our services, particularly for diagnosing and fixing rendering issues. We analyze the diagrams generated with our plugin only when there are issues with rendering, in order to deliver a better experience.
